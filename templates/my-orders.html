Frequently Asked Questions

What email should I use?

Use the same email address you provided during checkout. Your orders are linked to this email.

Where can I find my account credentials?

Your account credentials are shown immediately after payment on the success page. For security reasons, they are not stored for later viewing.

Can I view my password again?

For security, passwords are only shown once right after purchase. Please save them immediately. If you lost them, contact support with your order number.

How long are orders stored?

Your orders remain in our system indefinitely. You can always come back to view your purchase history.
